When I do:

slidify("index.Rmd"), I get a valid html document (ie No "compile" errors).  However, I always get a blank page at the end.

My index.Rmd file has six slides
Title Page

## Slide 1
--- 

to 

## Slide 5
--- 

When I view the .html document it says there are 7 pages and the last one is always blank.

I always end with:

--- 
(blank line)

I have tried removing the blank line and I get the following message(s):

Warning messages:
1: In readLines(doc, ...) : incomplete final line found on 'index.Rmd'
2: In readLines(doc, ...) : incomplete final line found on 'index.Rmd'


Any thoughts would be appreciated!  Thanks in advance.

Anonymous· 6 days ago 
Let's see, at the end of my presentation, I put a blank line, then the three dashes and a carriage return.  That caused it to put a blank (page 6.).

Next, I took out the final carriage return so the file ends exactly at the end of the three dashes.  I got:
Warning messages:
1: In readLines(doc, ...) : incomplete final line found on 'index.Rmd'
2: In readLines(doc, ...) : incomplete final line found on 'index.Rmd'

Next, I took out the three dashes and left the cursor on that line, as one ready to be typed on.  It went back to five pages.  I would say take out the three dashes at the very end.
